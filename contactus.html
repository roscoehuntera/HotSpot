About Our Project

HotSpot is a interactive social website for people to share their travel experiences and to inspire other users who are planning their own vacations. Travelers are able to find specifically what they are looking for when planning their vacation. On the website travelers are able to explore things like restaurants, hotels, activities, and many more. Users are able to post their own experience and react to other people’s experiences by liking, saving, and commenting on other users’ posts.
